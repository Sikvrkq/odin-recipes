Tortillas

Description

Tortilla is a dish originated from mexico that contains meat and vegetables covered in tortilla

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Beans
  • Salad
  • Spicy chilli sauce
  • Any meat you want

Steps

  1. Place tortilla on a table
  2. Heat the meat in the pan
  3. Slice all vegetables you have
  4. Add sauce on the tortilla
  5. Add heated meat mixed with vegetable
  6. Roll tortilla
  7. Place rolled tortilla in a toaster
